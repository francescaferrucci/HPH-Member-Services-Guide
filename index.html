Check-In Protocol

Basic Formula

Appt Type, Time: Reason for Visit - Presenting Complaint
Example: Medical 14:00: Recheck - Liver Values

Key Rules:

  • Reason for visit is what category was selected while booking
  • Time is always entered in military format
  • Pet's labeled time should be appointment time, NOT check-in time

Check-In Types

Drop-Offs (AM/PM)

Format: D/O AM/PM: Reason for Visit - Presenting Complaint
Example: D/O AM: Wellness - Health Certificate
Action: Admit into 'Ready for Exam'

Walk-Ins

Format: W/I [time]: Reason for Visit - Presenting complaint
Example: W/I 14:05: Injury - Torn Toenail
Action: Admit into 'Ready for Exam', highlight (Pet Parent Waiting)

Medical Appointments

Format: Medical [time]: Reason for Visit - Presenting Complaint
Example: Medical 09:00: Wellness - Annual PE/Vx Update
Action: Leave in Outpatient, Page TC or Send Slack to notify
Late Arrivals: If 10+ minutes late, ask Nurse if can still be seen. If not, Nurse will advise drop-off or walk-in.

Urgent Care

Check in as: U/C, time: Reason for Visit - Presenting Complaint
Action: Admit into Ready for Exam, Nurse triage before kenneling
After 5pm (HE2 only): Move into HE2 ER report, bring to ER Nurse

Surgery/Dentals

Format: SX/Dental (include details)
Example: SX - K9 Spay
Action: Admit into Ready for Procedure
Note: If after 7:30 AM, check with Nurse

QOL & Euthanasia

  • Check in as QOL with appointment time
  • Place in CLEANED exam room
  • Notify DVM, Medical Appointment Nurse, and/or TC
  • Make sure music is off!

Diagnostic Testing

Diagnostic Testing

A - ACTH Stimulation Test

  • Fasting not required
  • Pre draw + ONE hour post draw
  • Results typically same day

B - Bile Acids

  • MUST fast 12 hours prior
  • Pre draw + TWO hour post draw
  • Samples sent to reference lab
  • We call when results return

D - Low-Dose Dexamethasone Suppression Test

  • Fasting not required
  • Pre draw + FOUR hour + EIGHT hour post
  • Results typically same day
LATEST DROP-OFF FOR LDDST IS 10 AM! (Earlier is better)

Vomiting/Diarrhea Page 6

Questions to Ask:

  • When was Pet last 100% normal?
  • When did symptoms start?
  • Frequency of vomiting/diarrhea?
  • Last time Pet ate/drank?
  • Description of vomit/stool?
  • Possible ingestion of foreign material?
  • Other Pets in house with symptoms?
Next Step: Fast 24h ‚Üí Bland diet (chicken + rice) ‚Üí Call if persists

Toxicity Ingestion Page 6

  • What did Pet eat? (Have container?)
  • How much? When?
  • Eating/drinking normally?
  • Regular BMs and urination?
  • Other symptoms?
Next Step: Send to medical for immediate callback

Eye/Nose Discharge Page 6

  • When did it start?
  • Color of discharge?
  • Frequency? Worse at certain times?
  • New issue or history of allergies?
  • Recent contact with other dogs?
Next Step: Ask M to send photo to medical email + note for callback

ADR (Ain't Doing Right) Page 6

  • Last time Pet was 100% normal?
  • Possible ingestion of something?
  • Vomiting/diarrhea noted?
  • Other Pets in house unwell?
  • Eating/drinking normally?
Next Step: Send to Hannah Help Line for callback
